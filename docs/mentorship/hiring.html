How to Post a Job

  1. Contact a mentor: Use the Discord #hiring-pipeline channel to connect with a volunteer mentor who can review your posting.
  2. Submit job details: Provide role title, required skills, experience level, and compensation range.
  3. Get reviewed: Mentor reviews the posting for clarity and fairness.
  4. Post to Discord: Approved jobs are posted in the #job-board channel for community members.

Candidate Screening Checklist

For Employers: Screening Criteria

  • Portfolio check: Does the candidate have at least 2 complete projects on GitHub?
  • Technical skills: Can they write basic SQL queries (SELECT, JOIN, GROUP BY)?
  • Communication: Can they explain their project clearly in writing?
  • Cultural fit: Are they aligned with our team values and work style?

For Candidates: Preparation Checklist

  • Resume: Updated with relevant skills and project descriptions
  • GitHub: Clean repo structure with README files for each project
  • LinkedIn: Optimized headline and About section
  • Portfolio: At least 2 complete projects with documentation
  • Certifications: Free certs from Microsoft Learn, Google Analytics, etc.
