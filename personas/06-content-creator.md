## Background & Story

Sandy Lauguico is a YouTube educator and data science content creator who contributes to the DEP community. She's part of the **0.4% in content-related roles as a primary data role**, but she's willing to volunteer: **7.3% willing to "regularly post content"** and **38.8% willing to share knowledge**.

She creates YouTube tutorials (65.8% of community learns via YouTube) and has built an audience through consistent content creation. She wants more visibility for her work but doesn't know how to reach people beyond organic social media posting. She's frustrated by the gap between her 24.6% YouTube channel awareness and the 65.8% of community that learns via YouTube — a massive under-indexing opportunity.

She creates solo content, rarely cross-promotes with other creators, and wants recognition for contributions but has no platform to showcase them. She's part of GitHub handle @sclauguico, with 46 followers and 74 public repos including portfolio site, etl-geospatial (3⭐), and ecommerce-modern-data-stack (13⭐).
