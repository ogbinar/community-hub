## Background & Story

Jose graduated with a Math degree 6 months ago and immediately started looking for data roles. He's actively seeking employment: **86.9% of Job Seekers want upskilling** — the highest motivation-to-enrollment ratio of any segment in the community. He knows LinkedIn is where jobs are found (74.4% of employed use LinkedIn) but his profile is thin on projects and certifications.

He's frustrated by the gap between "I know Excel" and "I'm job-ready." He applies to jobs blindly, gets ghosted, and doesn't know what projects actually impress hiring managers. He uses Facebook (27.0%) more than LinkedIn (15.2%) for job discovery — but knows LinkedIn is where professionals actually find roles.

He wants upskilling (86.9%) but doesn't know which skills have the highest ROI or how to build a portfolio that stands out.

## Pain Points (Quantified)

### 1. Portfolio Gap
> "I don't have a portfolio. I apply to jobs on LinkedIn but I keep getting rejected."

- **Evidence:** No clear guidance on what projects actually impress hiring managers; Job Seekers are the second-largest segment (21.2%) with highest motivation-to-enroll ratio (86.9%)
- **Impact:** Applies to jobs blindly, gets ghosted; needs structured portfolio building but doesn't know how

### 2. LinkedIn Optimization Gap
> "I have a LinkedIn profile but it's empty. What should I put there?"

- **Evidence:** 74.4% of employed professionals use LinkedIn for job discovery — significantly higher than Career Shifters (56.1%)
- **Impact:** Profile is thin on projects and certifications; needs project-rich GitHub + LinkedIn combo but doesn't know how to build it

### 3. Under-Utilized Free Resources
> "I don't even know Microsoft Learn or Google Cloud Skills Boost exist."

- **Evidence:** Microsoft Learn at 8.6% (only 6.1% of Students use it); Google Cloud Skills Boost at 2.3%; HackerRank/LeetCode at 5.4%
- **Impact:** Doesn't know these resources exist or how to use them for credentials; missing out on free, industry-recognized certifications

### 4. DAX Knowledge Gap
> "I don't even know what DAX is."

- **Evidence:** Only 2.2% of Students use DAX vs 20.6% of Professionals — a meaningful differentiator for Data Analyst roles
- **Impact:** Missing a high-value upskill opportunity that separates capable Power BI users from experts (29.1% of Pros use it)
