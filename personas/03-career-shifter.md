## Background & Story

Lia worked in marketing communications for 4 years before realizing she wanted to transition into data. She's still in the "decision to shift" phase: **62.5% of Shifters are actively weighing their shift**. She earns ₱20k/month and wants more but feels trapped at the entry salary level.

She has no computing degree (58.5% don't) and feels like a liability to employers. She's motivated by career advancement (86.3% have high motivation to upskill) but is lagging on AI adoption — only 50.2% use AI daily for work vs 65.3% of Professionals. She uses Facebook more than LinkedIn for job discovery (27.0% vs 15.2%) — but knows LinkedIn is where professionals actually find roles.

She's frustrated by the Power BI gap (25-point gap between her 43.9% and Pros at 69.1%) and doesn't know what skills will actually get her to ₱50k+. She needs a "Local to Cloud Bridge" curriculum — integrating Cloud and AI as the monitoring/deployment layer of local work, not treating them as later stages.

## Pain Points (Quantified)

### 1. No Recognized Credential
> "I don't have a CS degree, so I feel like everyone's ahead of me."

- **Evidence:** 58.5% have NO computing degree — non-CS backgrounds dominate this segment
- **Impact:** Feels like a liability to employers; imposter syndrome is pervasive

### 2. Salary Trap
> "I'm earning ₱20k and want more, but I don't know what skills will actually get me to ₱50k."

- **Evidence:** 66.5% earn ≤₱35k/month; lowest satisfaction profile (72.9% score 1-5 on satisfaction)
- **Impact:** Structural dissatisfaction at entry level; salary-driven satisfaction is the #1 driver (ML analysis confirms)

### 3. AI Adoption Lag
> "I use ChatGPT sometimes, but I don't know how to integrate it into my work."

- **Evidence:** Only 50.2% use AI daily for work (vs 65.3% Pros); 15.4% use AI monthly or less; 3.4% say they don't plan to use AI (highest refusal rate)
- **Impact:** Lagging on AI adoption velocity compared to Professionals and Students

### 4. Power BI + DAX Gap
> "I know some Power BI, but I don't know DAX. How do I close that gap?"

- **Evidence:** Power BI gap: 25-point (69.1% DA Pros vs 43.9% Shifters); DAX gap: highest-signal gap (29.1% Pros vs 6.2% Shifters)
- **Impact:** Missing high-value upskills that separate capable users from experts

### 5. Cloud Usage Mismatch
> "I use GCP free tiers, but I know companies want AWS or Azure."

- **Evidence:** Less use AWS (25.8% vs 45.2% Pros) and Azure (31.5% vs 49.1% Pros) but more use GCP at 60.7% (vs 26.8% Data Pros)
- **Impact:** May be using free tiers but industry expects AWS/Azure familiarity
