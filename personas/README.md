## Each Persona Document Contains

For each of the 8 personas, the document includes:

1. **Profile Overview** — Name, age, location, education, role, device, salary, AI usage
2. **Background & Story** — Narrative describing who they are and why they joined DEP
3. **Demographics (Survey-Validated)** — Quantified metrics from DEP Survey 2026 with source citations
4. **Goals & Motivations** — Primary and secondary goals driving their behavior
5. **Pain Points (Quantified)** — Evidence-backed pain points with survey statistics
6. **Key Behaviors** — Learning habits, tool preferences, job search behavior
7. **Quotes & Verbatim Insights** — Realistic quotes based on survey findings
8. **Needs & Requirements for Community Hub** — Prioritized list of needs with survey validation
9. **How Might We Statements** — Actionable problem framing for ideation
10. **Community Hub Feature Recommendations** — MVP and supporting features with scores
11. **Success Metrics** — Measurable targets tied to survey baselines
