How It Works

1

Mentors Commit

One 30-min session per month on a specific day (e.g., first Thursday at 7 PM PHT).

Mentees Request

Choose a topic and request a session. No pressure, no commitment beyond the 30 minutes.

3

Mentor confirms availability. Session scheduled via Discord voice channel or Google Meet.

4

Session Happens

Focused 30-min session on your specific topic. Follow-up questions via Discord.
